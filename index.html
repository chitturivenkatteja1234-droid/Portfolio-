How I approach a build

Four things I hold to on every project, regardless of size.

  1. 1
    Semantic markup first.

    Real <header>, <nav>, <main>, <article>, and <footer> elements, not endless <div> soup — so the structure carries meaning on its own.

  2. 2
    ARIA where HTML alone falls short.

    Labels, roles, and live regions to keep custom interactions readable by assistive tech.

  3. 3
    SEO baked in, not bolted on.

    Descriptive titles, meta descriptions, structured data, and a logical heading outline.

  4. 4
    Keyboard-first testing.

    If it doesn't work cleanly with Tab, Shift+Tab, and Enter, it isn't done.
